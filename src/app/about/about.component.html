TFGFinder GEI

The collaborative platform for managing, searching, and offering Final Degree Project proposals.

Purpose

Streamline the process of finding and approving Final Degree Project proposals for students, professors, and organizations, centralizing all information in a single web environment.

Features

  • User registration and login (students, professors, organizations).
  • Proposal publication and editing.
  • Search proposal by keyword, category, and author.
  • Interest management and approvals.
  • And more to come...
